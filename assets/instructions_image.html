About this HIT:

  • Please only participate in this HIT if you know have clear color vision.

  • It should take about 5 minutes.

  • You will take part in an experiment involving images. You'll see a series of pairs of images and a reference image at the top. Your task is to determine which image has the reference image represented in it better.

  • You will complete a short practice (less than 1 minute) before starting the main task.
